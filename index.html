about.

meet arouney sithtaphone

Hi! I'm Arouney Sithtaphone - a senior at Woonsocket High School, a STEAM diversity advocate, and future engineer.

I am a current senior at Woonsocket High School in Woonsocket, RI. I am most interested in the intersection of STEAM, social justice, and business.

I grew up with great role models, but not everyone is afforded that opportunity. I believe that representation matters and that every young person should be able to see someone like them in whatever field they may choose. I hope to be an engineer that many little girls can look up to and envision themselves as. I love the limitless applications of STEAM and believe in using it to reimagine the current systems in which we live. Let's use STEAM to make our current systems more just and equitable.

My recent focus has been creating accessibility and racial equity in my community. The Harbour Youth Center in Woonsocket, RI has a special place in my heart for the countless hours of work I've been able to put in with them to provide teens in RI with real work experience. It was through the Youth Center that I was able to find my love and passion in serving others and providing valuable STEM resources to my community. Since then, I have partnered with countless other organizations. If you would like to know more, feel free to contact me or download my CV. Thank you for your interest!
